Quail Sprint San Diego

The day after the jQuery San Diego conference, we'll be holding an all-day sprint to work on the Quail issue queue. Specific areas we need help on are:

  • Internationalization.
  • Documentation cleanup, website work.
  • Copy-editing our test definitions.
  • Giving feedback on our documentation and training materials.

No JavaScript/jQuery experience is needed, we need people with Assistive Technology experience, general web backgrounds, or strong writing and communication skills to make the next release of Quail even better!

Join us Online

Can't make the sprint physically? We'll also be on #quail on Freenode, and a Google Hangout we'll link to from this page.

9am to 4pm
